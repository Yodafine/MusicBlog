About Me

Hi! I'm 'Yodafine', I'm 17 and this is the first website I've ever made!
I created this website for my webdesign class, and my idea was to create a blog where I write music album reviews.
I hope that by making this blog I'll learn and better understand how to code, develop and design a good-looking website.
I also hope that by writing reviews for this blog I can improve my writing skills, musical understanding, as well as help others discover new music(and maybe even connect with some music lovers!).

"Music can change the world."
